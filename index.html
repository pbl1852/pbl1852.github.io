Genre-Based Findings

Each genre attracted viewers through different emotional values and narrative elements.

ROMANCE

Emotional Immersion & Character Chemistry

Viewers responded strongly to attraction, relationship tension, dating choices, and sincere emotional storytelling. Romance becomes more appealing when viewers can emotionally invest in the characters.

chemistrytensionsincerity
Key insight: Romance content works best when emotional connection feels natural, not forced.
THRILLER / CRIME

Suspense, Survival & Curiosity

Thriller and crime contents gained attention through unpredictable situations, survival pressure, mystery, and constant curiosity about what happens next.

suspensesurvivalmystery
Key insight: Viewers stay engaged when tension rises step by step.
HUMAN / SOCIAL DRAMA

Empathy, Realism & Social Meaning

Human and social dramas attracted viewers by showing realistic problems, relatable emotions, and conflicts that reflect society or everyday relationships.

empathyrealismmessage
Key insight: Audiences value stories that feel close to real life.
COMEDY

Relatability, Timing & Everyday Humor

Comedy reactions focused on realistic situations, familiar relationship patterns, and lines that viewers could connect to their own daily lives.

relatablefunnyreal-life
Key insight: Comedy becomes stronger when viewers feel, “I have seen this before.”

From Comments to Strategy

This project shows that viewer reactions are not random. Romance is driven by emotional immersion, thriller by suspense, social drama by empathy, and comedy by relatability. Therefore, StreamKUS should not only choose popular genres, but also design clear emotional experiences that match why audiences watch and share content.
